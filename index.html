Services

Generally built using 2x8 pieces of wood and is used as the base of all concrete foundations for the wall to stand on. It's the floorplan before we put up the concrete forms and connect the two using rebar and a key marked into the footing. Footings also include concrete slabs and pads.

Construction of a single house foundation using forms. We utilize "Advance" forms, typically 8 feet tall, to pour the walls of the house. While the average size of a single house is around 200 square meters, we can also accommodate custom-sized houses based on proper blueprints which can require 9 or 10 foot tall panels. The process involves building, prepping, pouring, and stripping the panels, which usually takes 1-2 days.

Blocks include multiple houses within a foundation. These houses share central walls and can be arranged in two ways: side-by-side alignment (2-4 houses), or back-to-back alignment (3-4 houses on each side of a central wall). For block construction, we pour one side of the block at a time, adding insulation and rebar to the central wall. After pouring one side, we strip the panels and proceed to build, prep, and pour the second side, connecting it to the first. On average, constructing a block takes 3 days for a 3-4 house block and 4-5 days for a back-to-back block.

Our concrete repair services encompass various options. We specialize in concrete reinforcement, which involves cutting and pouring new sections of concrete with additional rebar for added strength. We also offer window replacement, where we resize the concrete wall hole, install new windows, then patch and waterproof the area. Additionally, our general concrete cutting services include walk-outs, stair cut-outs, beam pocket cutting, and garage door cutting. We can also assist with anchor bolt replacements or adjustments.

General patching services using ready-mix cement for the exterior of foundations, as well as the interior of porches and garages that share walls with the house. Additionally, we offer installation of Platon Foundation Wrap, a durable High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) membrane with a dimpled structure. This membrane prevents wet soil from reaching the foundation wall, and any moisture that gets past the membrane is directed to the foundation drain through an air gap drainage path. Platon is commonly used to waterproof block walls, poured concrete walls, and existing foundations.
